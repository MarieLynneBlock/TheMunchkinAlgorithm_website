Let us introduce ourselves

We are curious minds who happen to speak code. We operate in the space where logic meets creativity. Training smart models to think, but also teaching people to dream.

To us, technology is a tool, but human connection is the goal. Whether we are building an app, leading a workshop, or designing sound, we believe that the best algorithms have a heartbeat. We don't just optimise for efficiency; we optimise for connection and impact.

Our main services

Intelligent Systems

We don't just use AI; we engineer it to serve you. From fine-tuning custom language models to building autonomous agents, we create smart systems that automate the mundane and amplify your human creativity.

Creative Coding & Web

The web shouldn't be static. We craft reactive, generative, and immersive digital experiences. Using modern frameworks, we turn standard websites into living, breathing digital art.

Sound Design & Audio Production

Sound tells the stories that visuals can't. Whether it is producing a professional podcast or music, composing, or designing an audio identity for your brand, we ensure you are heard with impact.

Workshops & Coaching

Technology moves fast; we help you keep up. We facilitate hands-on workshops to demystify complex tech and AI models. We ignite team curiosity and empower creative individuals to move from users to makers.

Live Algorithms & Performance

Code is our instrument. We bring algorithms to the stage through live-coded music and real-time visuals. We turn the hidden logic of programming into a visceral, audiovisual experience that unfolds in the moment.

Strategy & Vision

Innovation needs a map. We help organisations and creative individuals navigate the AI era. Whether defining a strategic roadmap or integrating AI into your artistic workflow, we ensure that every technological step you take serves a real purpose.

recent projects

  • Sonic Alchemy Lab

    Digital workbench for audio analysis. From basic waveforms to complex Fourier transforms, these notebooks provide a research-ready environment for exploring the science of sound.

  • Harmony in Algorithmic Beauty

    A research exploration into the synergy of generative algorithms. This project utilises L-Systems to drive simultaneous visual and musical generation, investigating the algorithmic bond between sight and sound.

  • DAW Audio Plugins

    A comprehensive collection of custom audio plugins built with C++ and Python. These tools integrate intelligent AI functions to optimize production workflows and automate complex tasks across modern DAWs.

  • Playable Sound Machines

    A fusion of creative coding and interface design. This collection features algorithmic drum machines, interactive synthesizers, and web-based instruments, all wrapped in beautiful, accessible UIs for musicians and non-musicians alike.

    A collection of algorithmic musical studies composed entirely in the ChucK programming language. These etudes explore real-time sound synthesis and procedural composition, playable directly in miniAudicle.
